## PAYMENT & PARTNERSHIP MODELS

### Option 1: Full Payment Discount
- Pay in full, receive 15% discount
- Best if you have funding/savings

### Option 2: Payment Plans (0% Interest)
- Spread across 3-12 months
- No interest, no hidden fees
- Cancel anytime (forfeit uncompleted services)

### Option 3: Revenue Share Hybrid
- Artist pays 40% upfront
- SWRV takes 10% of revenue for 12 months to recover remaining 60%
- Best for artists with early traction/existing audience

### Option 4: Equity Partnership (Enterprise Only)
- SWRV invests services in exchange for 5-10% ownership
- Requires significant business potential
- Ongoing partnership and profit-sharing
- Very limited availability

### Option 5: Performance-Based Pricing
- "You don't pay us until your first sync deal"
- SWRV takes 20% of first 3 sync deals in exchange for setup & coaching
- Artist pays $0 upfront, gets full ecosystem built
