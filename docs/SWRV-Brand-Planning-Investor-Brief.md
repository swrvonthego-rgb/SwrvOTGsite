## THE CORE SERVICE: VISION MAPPING & REVERSE ENGINEERING

### What is Reverse Engineering of Vision?

Most businesses start with a product, then find customers.

SWRV **starts with vision**, then works backward to identify every single thing necessary to fulfill that vision.

**Example: Creating a Music Video for an Artist**

To create ONE music video, you need:
1. Beat/instrumental production
2. Songwriting & lyric creation
3. Recording studio time
4. Vocal technique & performance coaching (BIRDSONG Method)
5. Audio engineering & mixing/mastering
6. Music video production design
7. Director & cinematographer
8. Post-production editing & color grading
9. Building the artist brand/aesthetic
10. Photography & documentary content during process
11. Social media strategy & rollout plan
12. Business/LLC structure for payment receipt
13. Banking & accounting setup
14. Legal protection (contracts, rights, IP ownership)
15. Marketing & promotion strategy

**One vision → 15+ interconnected services**

When we reverse engineer, we see the ENTIRE ecosystem needed, not just the final product.

## THE FAIRNESS MODEL: Empowering Artists While Building Business

### Our Problem with Traditional Models

Traditional deals:
- Give most control to labels/investors
- Require artists to sacrifice ownership
- Leave artists vulnerable & dependent
- Create predatory payment structures
- Don't build artist credit or independence

### The SWRV Model: Shared Growth

**ARTIST OWNS:**
- All creative work produced
- Master recordings
- Publishing rights to original compositions
- Brand assets created
- Their business entity
- Their customer relationships

**SWRV RECEIVES:**
- Service fees for direct services (studio time, coaching, etc.)
- Percentage of revenue (10%) during growth phase as investment recovery
- Ongoing partnership (optional) including:
  - Project-based collaboration fees
  - Ecosystem maintenance & growth
  - Continued business/branding support
- Referral network (both directions)
- Right of first refusal on future projects

**ARTIST BUILDS:**
- Personal credit (through our credit-building program)
- Business credit (through LLC & payment structure)
- Access to business lines of credit
- Asset ownership
- Long-term sustainable business
- Skills & capabilities for ongoing independence

### Payment Flexibility Models

**Option 1: Performance-Based**
- SWRV invests services upfront
- Receives 10% of artist revenue (streaming, sales, sync licensing) for 24-36 months
- Artist keeps 90% + independent control
- Artist can buy out SWRV's percentage at any time

**Option 2: Equity-Based**
- SWRV takes 15-20% revenue share permanently
- Artist receives strategic business partnership ongoing
- Both parties benefit from long-term growth
- Clear governance & decision-making structure

**Option 3: Fee-Based (Artist-Funded)**
- Artist pays for services directly
- Vision Mapping: $500
- Recording Package: $2,000-5,000
- Full Ecosystem Build: $10,000-25,000
- Artist retains 100% of all revenue
- SWRV provides services only (no ongoing partnership)

**Option 4: Hybrid (Volume Discount)**
- Combination of fees + revenue share
- Artist pays reduced fees upfront
- SWRV takes 5-7% revenue share as ongoing partnership
- Best for artists with existing audience

### The Credit-Building Advantage

SWRV can do something labels cannot:
- Help artists establish business credit
- Guide credit score improvement
- Access business lines of credit
- Build financial independence
- Navigate banking as a business owner

This creates **artist autonomy** rather than artist dependency.
