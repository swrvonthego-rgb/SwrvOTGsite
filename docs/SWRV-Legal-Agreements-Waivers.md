## 4. PROTECTION CLAUSE: FAIR COMPENSATION & ARTIST RIGHTS

### 4.1 Artist Ownership Guarantee

**SWRV explicitly guarantees that:**

1. **Artist owns all music created** - Full ownership of compositions, lyrics, and recordings with no reversion to SWRV
2. **Artist owns all brand assets** - Photos, videos, logos, visual identity, website content
3. **Artist keeps 100% of revenue** (unless revenue-share option selected) - All streaming, sales, sync, and sponsorship income
4. **Artist controls their work** - Artist has final say on how work is released, distributed, and marketed
5. **Artist can leave anytime** - No long-term exclusive contracts or non-compete clauses

### 4.2 Fair Payment Structure

**If SWRV takes revenue share, Artist receives:**
- Transparent monthly accounting
- Detailed breakdown of all revenue sources
- Verification rights to audit SWRV's records
- Right to terminate revenue-share arrangement with 90-day notice
- Ability to buy out SWRV's ownership stake

**If Artist pays SWRV directly:**
- Clear itemized invoices
- Transparent hourly or project rates
- No hidden fees or surprise charges
- Money-back guarantee if unsatisfied with services (within first 2 weeks)

### 4.3 Credit & Attribution

**SWRV credits Artist for:**
- Creating the music and artistic vision
- Being the driving force of the brand
- Building the audience and community
- Generating the revenue and success

**SWRV takes credit ONLY for:**
- Professional services provided
- Methodology and coaching
- Business infrastructure support
- Technical execution

**Public Credit Language:**
> "[Song/Project Name] was [created by / performed by / written by] [Artist Name], developed with the SWRV Team."

### 4.4 No Predatory Clauses

**This agreement specifically excludes:**
- 360-degree deals (SWRV taking cuts of all Artist income)
- Reversion clauses (ownership reverting to SWRV if Artist ceases activity)
- Non-compete clauses (preventing Artist from working with other producers/services)
- Exclusivity clauses (requiring Artist to work only with SWRV)
- Perpetual rights (rights lasting beyond artist the lifetime of the Artist)
- Unconscionable payment terms (Artist paying unsustainable percentages)

## 5. DISPUTE RESOLUTION & TERMINATION

### 5.1 Dispute Resolution Process

**If disagreement arises between Artist and SWRV:**

**Step 1: Direct Conversation (14 days)**
- Artist and primary SWRV contact attempt to resolve in person/video call
- Both parties state concerns clearly
- Document agreement or remaining disagreement

**Step 2: Mediation (14-30 days)**
- If unresolved, both parties agree to mediation
- Third-party mediator (mutually selected) facilitates
- Mediation costs split equally
- Goal: Find mutually acceptable resolution

**Step 3: Arbitration (If needed)**
- If mediation fails, dispute goes to binding arbitration
- Arbitrator selected by mutual agreement
- Each party presents case
- Arbitrator's decision is final and binding
- Arbitration location: [City, State]

**Legal Jurisdiction:**
- This agreement governed by laws of [State/Country]
- Both parties agree to arbitration vs. litigation
- Both parties waive right to jury trial

### 5.2 Termination Rights

**Artist can terminate this agreement:**
- At any time with written notice
- Effective immediately for service-based payments
- Effective 30 days out for revenue-share arrangements (to allow final accounting)
- Artist retains all created materials and ownership

**SWRV can terminate this agreement:**
- For non-payment (after 14-day notice and Artist non-response)
- For Artist's unauthorized use of materials
- If Artist uses SWRV services for illegal purposes
- By mutual written agreement

**Upon Termination:**
- Artist receives final accounting within 30 days
- SWRV returns all Artist materials and recordings
- Both parties cease use of each other's confidential information
- Previous work remains Artist's property in perpetuity

### 5.3 Post-Termination Obligations

After termination:
- SWRV may continue crediting Artist with "Developed with SWRV" in portfolio/case studies
- Artist may use SWRV's name as reference ("I worked with SWRV")
- Both parties continue respecting confidentiality
- Revenue-share agreements (if selected) terminate completely
